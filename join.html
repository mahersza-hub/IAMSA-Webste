Choose your path.

Select the application that fits you. All forms are reviewed within 5 working days.

UK Regional Lead Application

One regional lead per UK medical school. You are the IAMSA presence at your institution — organising local events, recruiting members, and connecting your school to the national network. Commitment: 2–4 hours per month.

National Committee Application

Open roles: Vice President, Research Lead, Education Lead, Events Lead, Communications Lead, International Liaison. Commitment: 4–8 hours per month. Leadership experience preferred but not required.

International Representative Application

Establish IAMSA's presence in your country — Ireland, USA, Canada, Australia, Germany, Singapore, or propose a new country. Build the local chapter network. Named international representative on all IAMSA communications.

Why Join

What membership gives you.

📚

Full Education Hub Access

All webinar recordings, workshop slides, specialty AI libraries, and the IAMSA curriculum framework — all free to members.

🔬

Research Opportunities

Apply to join the research working group. Get matched to live IAMSA research projects with named authorship on publications.

🎟️

Free Event Access

All national webinars, workshops, and the annual IAMSA National Conference — free to members, with certificates of attendance.

🤝

Mentorship Programme

Apply to be matched with a clinical or research mentor from the IAMSA advisory panel when the programme launches July 2026.

📬

Monthly Newsletter

The IAMSA Monthly — society updates, new research, upcoming events, and AI in clinical practice news. Delivered to your inbox.

🌍

National & International Network

Access the IAMSA member network — peers at every UK medical school and internationally. Collaborate, connect, and build together.
