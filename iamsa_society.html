Why We Exist

Medicine is changing.
Students aren't prepared.

Over 75% of medical students receive no formal AI education in their curriculum. Meanwhile, 2 in 3 physicians now use AI in clinical practice — a figure that doubled in just two years.

The NHS has invested over £150 million through the NHS AI Lab. Regulatory frameworks are evolving. Clinical AI tools are in wards, radiology suites, and pathology labs — right now.

IAMSA exists to close this gap. Clinically grounded, peer-delivered, internationally connected. Not a tech club. Not a theory society. A community of future doctors preparing for the medicine they will actually practice.

Roadmap

Launch plan.
June to December 2026.

A phased, strategic launch built for national reach and international scale from day one.

1
Now → June 2026
Foundation Phase — Build Before Launch
Establish the society structure, register the organisation, build the website, recruit founding chapter leads at target medical schools, and secure founding partnerships. Design the brand. Draft the constitution. Build the social presence.
Society Registration Website Launch Brand Identity Founding Team Recruitment Social Media Setup Partnership Outreach
2
June 2026
Official Launch — UK National
Public launch with founding announcement, chapter leads from 10+ UK medical schools confirmed, founding webinar series announced, and launch event at the University of Sunderland. Press release to medical student press — The Medic Portal, Student BMJ, NANSIG.
Launch Event 10+ Chapter Leads First Webinar Press Coverage Student BMJ Submission
3
July → September 2026
National Expansion — All UK Medical Schools
Recruit chapter leads at all 33 UK medical schools. Launch monthly webinar series. Establish research working group. Submit CPD accreditation application for first conference. Begin IFMSA partnership conversations for international expansion.
33 UK Chapters Monthly Webinars Research Working Group CPD Application IFMSA Outreach
4
October → December 2026
First National Conference & International Pilot
Host inaugural IAMSA National Conference — CPD accredited, abstract submissions, keynote clinicians and AI researchers. Launch pilot international chapters in Ireland, USA, and Australia. Publish first annual needs assessment survey on AI education in UK medical schools.
Inaugural Conference International Pilot — IE, US, AUS Needs Assessment Publication Mentorship Programme Launch
5
2027 Onwards
International Scale
Full international chapter network across Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific, and Africa. Annual International Medical AI Symposium. GMC curriculum consultation contribution. Position statement on AI in medical education. Industry partnership programme generating research funding.
